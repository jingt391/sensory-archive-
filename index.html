Tiny hands hold with pride, symbol of journey,

smiles so wide, A first step taken, with love inside.

A window to the world, a touch, a swipe, it comes alive,

Voices heard, yet miles apart, world of knowledge in your heart.

Petals soft, colors bright bloom, a pure delight.

Velvet hues and scents that twirl, gift of nature, hearts unfurl.

Cold beneath, a glide, silent swoosh.

Sharp and sleek, they carve the ice, sparkling trail beneath the sky.

A paper light, yet filled with weight,

symbol of hard work and fate. Years of toil, the tests, the nights.

Soft and cuddly, warm and sweet, gentle hug, a friend to meet.

A silent listener, soft and near, a world of cheer.

Keys that sing with gentle grace,

Ivory and black, fingers fly, the sounds take flight.

Tall and slender, rich and deep.

Wooden frame and strings so tight, low and mellow, bold and free.

Bound in covers, filled with worlds to wander through.

Turn the page and you will see, endless wonders, wild and free.

Loyal heart, watchful eyes, who never tells you lies.

A bark, a leap, a loving paw, a bond unbreakable and raw.

Bright and fine, a touch of gold, a sparkling shine.

Worn with pride or kept with care, alittle gem that whispers flair.

Small and round, soft and light, in baby's night.

One day left, no longer missed, replaced by words and bliss.

A perfect circle, small and bright,

Endless, timeless, strong it stays, a silent vow that never fades.

Floating high, drifting softly, feather-light.

Gone in whispers, lost so soon, like a vanished piece of moon.
